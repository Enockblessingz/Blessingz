Our Journey

The Love Story

Every great love is a sequence of beautiful moments, unexpected miracles, and memories created together. Here are ours.

2021

The First Meeting

A spilled coffee, a shared laugh, and an apology that turned into a two-hour conversation neither wanted to end. What started as an ordinary moment became the beginning of something extraordinary.

2022

A Quiet Friendship 🤍

For a year we were simply friends — sharing long walks, late-night conversations, laughter, and countless memories. Slowly, we realized every moment was better when shared together.

2023

Something More ❤️

On a rainy evening in October, friendship transformed into forever. There was no grand gesture — just a moment, a feeling, and two hearts realizing they had found something special.

2024

Growing Together 🌹

Through every challenge and every celebration, we learned that love is built with patience, trust, laughter, and choosing each other every single day.

2025

The Proposal 💍

Beneath the arches of the garden where our story began, came a question that changed everything. With a heart full of love and excitement, forever received its first promise.

2026

The Wedding

Surrounded by family, friends, and everyone who supported our journey, we begin the next chapter — not as two people meeting, but as two lives choosing each other forever.
